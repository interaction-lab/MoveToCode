Detailed Description

Provides a solver that constrains the target to a region safe for hand constrained interactive content.

This solver is intended to work with Microsoft.MixedReality.Toolkit.Input.IMixedRealityHand but also works with Microsoft.MixedReality.Toolkit.Input.IMixedRealityController.

Enumerator

◆ SolverRotationBehavior

enum Microsoft.MixedReality.Toolkit.Utilities.Solvers.HandConstraint.SolverRotationBehavior
strong

Specifies how the solver should rotate when tracking the hand.

Enumerator
None 

The solver simply follows the rotation of the tracked object.

LookAtMainCamera 

The solver faces the main camera (user).

LookAtTrackedObject 

The solver faces the tracked object.

A hand to world transformation is applied to work with traditional user facing UI (-z is forward).

◆ SolverSafeZone

enum Microsoft.MixedReality.Toolkit.Utilities.Solvers.HandConstraint.SolverSafeZone
strong

Specifies a zone that is safe for the constraint to solve to without intersecting the hand.

Safe zones may differ slightly from motion controller to motion controller, it's recommended to pick the safe zone best suited for your intended controller and application.

Enumerator
UlnarSide 

On the left controller with palm up, the area right of the palm.

RadialSide 

On the left controller with palm up, the area left of the palm.

AboveFingerTips 

Above the longest finger tips.

BelowWrist 

Below where the controller meets the arm.

AtopPalm 

Floating above the palm, towards the "inside" of the hand (opposite side of knuckles), based on the "down" vector of the palm joint (i.e., the grabbing-side of the hand)
